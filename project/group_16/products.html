Client Description

Our client is Sumanadasa Electronics & Electricals, one of the biggest shops in Ambalangoda which belongs to Sumanadasa Group Company. The main functions of the shop is to sell electrical items which are receiving from the agents and conducting a service center for repairing electrical items such as air-conditioners. At the moment they have only one showroom, which is located at Ambalangoda, but they are planning to build more showrooms in the nearby cities. The service center also provides various repairing services outside the center as well.
